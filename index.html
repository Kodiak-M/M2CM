Tokenomics

Here's how the MC2M token is distributed:

50% Presale

50% of tokens will be sold in the presale event to fund the development and marketing of the project.

25% Community

25% of tokens will be allocated to community rewards, staking, and farming.

15% Development

15% will go towards the development team and project operations.

10% Charity

10% of tokens will be donated to animal charities and welfare programs.
